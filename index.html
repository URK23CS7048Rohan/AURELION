About the Event

Mission

AURELION 2026 is a 24-hour national hackathon designed to inspire creative, AI-driven solutions to India's most pressing challenges in Food, Water, Health, and Energy.

Vision

Bringing together 100 teams from IITs, NITs, IIITs, and leading universities, the event fosters innovation, collaboration, and real-world problem solving.

Organizers

Organized by IEEE Computer Society Karunya Chapter & KITS ACM Student Chapter at Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences, Coimbatore.
